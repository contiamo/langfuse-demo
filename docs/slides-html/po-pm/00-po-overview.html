What Does "Good" Look Like?
Define Your Quality Criteria
Format  Working session · draft 2–3 quality criteria for your product  ·  → 02-quality-criteria.html
10:45 · 30 min · Working session · How do we know the AI is getting better?
The core question: what would need to be measurably true to say "we improved the AI this sprint"?
Signal, not feeling — faithfulness, completeness, tone, safety: a number you can track over time
Acceptance criteria don't fit probabilistic output — the PM version is quality criteria + dataset + threshold
Anatomy of a dimension: name · type (numeric / boolean / categorical) · what it rewards
Exercise: draft 2–3 score dimensions for your own product
These quality criteria are the artifact you'll annotate against and gate on all afternoon.
